How to Apply for Admissions

Follow these steps to apply for admissions through the official portals:

  1. Visit the official website of the institution or examination authority.
  2. Register yourself by providing basic details like name, email ID, and mobile number.
  3. Fill in the application form with all required personal, academic, and other details.
  4. Upload scanned copies of photographs, signatures, and required documents as per specifications.
  5. Pay the application fee through online payment modes like credit/debit card, net banking, UPI, etc.
  6. Submit the application and take a printout of the confirmation page for future reference.
  7. Keep tracking your application status and check for updates regularly.

Note: Always check the eligibility criteria, important dates, and document requirements before applying for any admission process.
